Pesto Lasagna Recipe

  • 16 oz package lasagna noodles
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 16 oz package frozen chopped spinach, thawed
  • 7 oz basil pesto
  • 30 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 9 oz Alfredo-style pasta sauce
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350° F (175° C).
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil Add lasagna and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente and drain.
  3. In large skillet over medium heat, sauté onion in olive oil until tender. Stir in spinach; remove from heat and stir in pesto.
  4. In a large bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, salt, pepper, and nutmeg.
  5. In a 3-quart greased baking dish, layer noodles, then spinach mixture, followed by ricotta mixture. Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese. Repeat the layers ending with noodles o n top. Spread Alfredo sauce on top of noodles and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
  6. Cover with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 45 to 55 minutes or until done.
